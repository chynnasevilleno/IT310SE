SOFTWARE ENGINEERING

is the application of a systematic, disciplined, quantifiable approach to the operation, development, and maintenace of software. It aims to develop and maintain systems that behave reliably and efficiently. These systems ought to be affordable when being developed and maintained. Above else, it must the systems and softwares made must satisfy all the requirements that customers have defined for them.
